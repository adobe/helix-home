### Agenda

> I hear this is a Brain Freeze, are you going to eat ice cream all day?

This is the SLICC track. We forked off the sliccathon from the Operations Workshop so it has its own room and its
own agenda. We'll keep it hands-on: instead of presentations, we'll let code settle arguments. There will be plenty
of programming, coding, and the occasional headache from shipping releases too fast.

The main purpose is social: get the Self-Licking Ice Cream Cone crowd in one place, onboard new contributors, and
work on stuff that won't fit neatly into a 45-minute Teams meeting.
