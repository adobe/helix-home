### Agenda

> The first one — what did the ice cream cone do for two days in Utah?

The inaugural Brain Freeze: two intense days at the Adobe Lehi campus to get the Self-Licking Ice Cream Cone crowd
in one room. Hands-on from the start — no presentations, code settled the arguments. Shorter than a full hackathon
week, but the release counter did not slow down for it.

### Location

> Where did this happen?

Adobe Lehi Office, Lehi, UT (the Utah campus, ~30 minutes south of Salt Lake City).

#### Travel

Fly into Salt Lake City (SLC); the Lehi campus is a short drive south down I-15. Provo (PVU) is closer if you can find a flight.

### Goals

> What did we set out to do?

Get SLICC into shape and into more hands: stabilize the Mac app and extension, sharpen the browser shell, and
make skills easy to install and discover.

#### Ideas / Suggestions / Topics Collection

- Mac app and `sliccstart` packaging and auto-update
- Browser shell command coverage (`git`, `node`, `python`, `playwright`)
- Skills install flow and read-only discovery across the reachable VFS
- Multi-agent delegation for parallel work
